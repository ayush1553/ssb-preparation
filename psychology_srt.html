🧠⚡ Situation Reaction Test (SRT) ⚡🧠

📖✨ 𝗜𝗻𝘁𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 ✨📖

In this test, 🧪 real-life situations are described, and the candidate must respond quickly and appropriately.

  • 🚫 No special intelligence is required — instead, the test checks:
    • 💭 Common sense
    • 🧠 Reasoning ability
    • 🧓 Maturity

Your answers reflect your true personality! This test shows how you react to life’s unexpected turns, using the 🌱 experience you've gained.

⏱️📄 𝗛𝗼𝘄 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗧𝗲𝘀𝘁 𝗶𝘀 𝗖𝗼𝗻𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗲𝗱 📄⏱️

  • 🗂️ You will be given 60 situations in a booklet.
  • 📝 You need to write your reactions on a separate answer sheet.
  • ⏳ Time allotted: 30 minutes only!

That means only 30 seconds per situation 😲 — not much time to think!
As a result, 💡 your natural thinking comes out — which reveals your real character.

🎯 To do well:

  • ✅ Practice regularly!
  • ✅ Be consistent — similar situations may appear again in different forms.
  • ✅ Don’t contradict your earlier responses.
  • ✅ Give complete actions in brief form — not just emotions!
💬 Example:
🕹️ Situation: Just before the start of a doubles match, he found his partner missing.
🗯️ He...
✔️ Reaction: He took the substitute player.
🏆 Complete Action: He took the substitute player, played the match, and won it.
