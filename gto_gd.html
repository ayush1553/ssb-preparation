🎭 GD (GROUP DISCUSSION)

📘 How the Test is Conducted

  • 🌀 The GD is the first activity in the GTO test series.
  • 🪑 Candidates sit in a circle in numerical order.
  • 🔁 Two rounds of GD are conducted.

✅ Round 1

  • Group is given two topics to choose from.
  • Discussion time: ⏳ 20 minutes
  • Topics are based on current affairs.

✅ Round 2

  • Only one topic is provided.
  • Time limit: ⏳ 15–20 minutes
🗞️ Tip: Stay updated with current affairs by reading newspapers and watching debates regularly!

💡 Sample GD Topics (🔥 Current Affairs)

🗨️ 1. Ongoing Civil Unrest in Egypt, Libya, Syria

What do you think is the major fallout of this?

  • 🔹 Will it lead to World War III?
  • 🔹 Will it disintegrate the Arab world?

🗨️ 2. Scams in India: Who’s Responsible?

India has seen many scams recently. Who’s to blame?

  • 🔹 Politicians
  • 🔹 Bureaucrats
  • 🔹 Common people

🗨️ 3. India’s Road to Superpower Status

Which is the most complex issue that must be tackled first?

  • 🔹 Terrorism
  • 🔹 Naxalism
  • 🔹 Separatism
